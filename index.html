The Outdoor Advocate Society is an organization promoting outdoor activities for people of all ages. We feel that humans need to spend time outdoors with nature for both their mental and physical well-being.

Turn off those TVs, computers, tablets, and cell phones. Spend some time exploring our national parks and monuments, mountains, trails, rivers, shores, and many more places.
